About me

My name is Vinicius Harteman Pena, I was born in August 1993 in the city of Araraquara/SP. Currently I live with my wife and two children, I am studying Computer Science at Anhembi Morumbi University (Laureate International Universities), and when I have a little time I like to play chess, which is my hobby.

My professional journey started at the age of 13, when I got my first job in a sewing studio, and at the age of 16, I studied Auto Mechanics at the Senai institution in Araraquara, and got my second job. At the age of 19, I volunteered to enlist in the Brazilian army, and served one year in the war shot of my city. Motivated by friends and family, I discovered how interesting it was to work in the area of ​​technology, but it was during my journey at college that this passion for the profession emerged.

Focused on learning and seeking to improve my knowledge, I am part of a study group among friends, where we share experiences and learning from our daily lives.
